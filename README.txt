This is the Acme-SF project, a software of a fictitious company that specialises in helping
organisations get started on a variety of topics related to the management and development of
software projects.

The goal of this project is to develop a WIS to help this organisation manage their
business, from projects to user stories, contracts, progress logs, and quality audits.
